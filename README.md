`(.*) Pelé` is a simple firstname finder app, based on a list of several thousands of old-fashioned French firstnames.

The app was "born" of a personal need for finding the first name of a certain baby...
It was used for almost 9 month to our great satisfaction, to very closely and efficiently examinate _all the firstnames_.
A short list was made, shared, discussed and the best firstname was ultimately chosen.
